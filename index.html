E komo mai!

Set out on a huakaʻi through Hawaiʻi. Each photo is a stop along the way. Find where it sits on the map: which mokupuni (island), which moku (district), which ahupuaʻa (land division).

A huakaʻi is a journey. The aim here is not to get every round right on the first try — it is to keep returning, see new places, and slowly come to know the land.

How it works

  • 3 quick rounds per game.
  • You see a photo. Tap the map to drop a pin in the right ahupuaʻa.
  • The map drills down for you: mokupuni → moku → ahupuaʻa.
  • Land on the right ahupuaʻa and that site is yours forever — your progress is saved.
  • Don't know one? Tap I don't know and the answer is shown on the map.
  • Goal: solve every site to beat the game.
